Welcome to L.I.H.F.S. We are a modern martial arts organization dedicated to studying, reconstructing and training with the German Longsword, LIHFS is a modern martial arts organization dedicated to studying, reconstructing and training with the German Longsword, the weapon central to Historical European Martial Arts (“HEMA”). We are a HEMA allience recognized organization teaching in the Liechtenauer tradition. Our program is intended to build a progressive understanding of the art and will be held at one of two levels – Novice and Intermediate. Once a student completes the Novice and Intermediate Modules, they will be invited to take the Haupstück (Entry Level) exam. If the student passes the exam, they will become a member and proceed to the Advanced Module.

CONTENT NEEDED!!!

Classes will begin with warm-ups and review of prior sessions, continue with new techniques, and finish with specific drills designed to refine the new techniques covered. This comprehensive longsword training program is based principally on the manuscript of 14th century German Master Johannes Liechtenauer, as interpreted by the LIHFS. In addition to our own interpretation of the Lichtenauer canon, including the commentary and interpretations by Sigmund Ringeck, Peter Von Danzig, Codex Döbringer (MS 3227a), Hans Talhoffer, Paulus Kal, Andres Lignitzer, and Ott Jud and others, we have consulted with and incorporated relevant interpretations and teachings of the leading modern day masters.

Be a Part of History:

Reconstructing Historical European Martial Arts(HEMA) LIHFS is a modern martial arts organization dedicated to studying, reconstructing and training with the German Longsword. LIHFS is not a re-enactment or LARP organization, nor a theatrical or "stage-fighting" group.
